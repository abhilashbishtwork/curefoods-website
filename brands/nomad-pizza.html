Nomad Pizza

Hand-stretched pizza, globally inspired.

Nomad Pizza brings artisanal, hand-stretched pizzas with globally inspired toppings to Curefoods' delivery network, positioned as a premium everyday-pizza alternative to the big chains.

The story

Nomad Pizza was built on a simple bet: Indian pizza delivery didn't need to mean assembly-line dough. Every base is hand-stretched to order, and the topping menu borrows from Nomad's travel-inspired name — Peri Peri, Korean BBQ, and classic Margherita sit on the same menu.

Founded: 2021
