Frozen Bottle

Thick shakes, waffles and dessert-in-a-bottle.

Frozen Bottle specialises in indulgent thick shakes, waffles and jar desserts, acquired into the Curefoods portfolio to round out its dessert and beverages layer.

The story

Frozen Bottle joined Curefoods as part of its dessert and beverages expansion, bringing a cult following for its bottle-served thick shakes. The brand runs both dine-in kiosks in malls and high streets, and delivery-only listings, making it one of the few Curefoods brands with a strong offline retail footprint.

Founded: 2016 (acquired by Curefoods 2021)
