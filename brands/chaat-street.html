Chaat Street

Street-side chaat, cloud-kitchen fresh.

Chaat Street brings India's street-food chaat culture — pani puri, bhel, aloo tikki — to delivery, as part of the Kitchens of EatFit family.
BengaluruHyderabad
Order on Swiggy Order on Zomato

The story

Chaat is notoriously hard to deliver well — Chaat Street's entire product design is built around packaging that keeps crunch separate from chutney until the last moment, so the chaat still tastes street-fresh at the door.

Founded: 2022
