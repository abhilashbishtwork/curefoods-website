Olio

Italian comfort food, kitchen-fresh.

Olio serves Italian-inspired comfort food — pizza, pasta and sides — as one of Curefoods' core delivery brands, with a leaner spinoff, Enso, exploring a Japanese-fusion adjacent concept.

The story

Olio focuses on the essentials of Italian comfort eating done consistently well: pizza and pasta cooked fresh per order rather than pre-batched. The brand's 'core' menu is what most cities see; select clusters also run Enso, a related concept the team uses to test new formats.

Questions about Olio

What is Enso and how is it related to Olio?
Enso is a related, smaller-format concept run out of select Olio kitchens as Curefoods tests new menu formats; it is not available in every city Olio serves.
